About ACE Academy

ACE — Academy for Cricket Excellence (Visakhapatnam) is a performance-driven cricket academy focused on building disciplined, skilled, and match-ready cricketers through structured coaching, real assessment, and consistent feedback.

Operated by Sri Lakshminarasimha Sports Academy • Based in Sontyam, Visakhapatnam
Our Mission

To give serious young cricketers a real platform — not just practice. We focus on fundamentals, fitness, match temperament, and long-term development so players are prepared for the next level.

What Makes ACE Different
Structured Coaching

Clear training plans, level-wise progression, and measurable improvement — not random nets every day.

Performance Mindset

Discipline, consistency, and match temperament are trained alongside technique.

Fair Opportunity

Selections and scholarships are merit-based. Talent gets noticed and evaluated properly.

What We Offer
  • Cricket coaching programs (beginner to advanced)
  • Nets sessions, skill assessments, and match simulations
  • Strength & conditioning support (as per batch requirements)
  • Clinics, camps, bootcamps, and special programs
  • Talent identification initiatives like ACE Talent Hunt
Our Coaching Philosophy

Great players are not built by motivation alone — they’re built by repetition + correction + match exposure. At ACE, players train with intent, get actionable feedback, and learn to perform under real pressure.

Who We Train

We train young cricketers who are serious about improvement — typically from Visakhapatnam and nearby areas, and also players who travel from outside the city. Programs are designed for different age groups and skill levels.
